# 7月14 每日 15 单词

## **estate**

地产

> My estate includes my house and part of the forest.
>
> 我的地产包括我的房子和这片森林的一部分。

## **intense**

强烈的，激烈的

> that was an intense thunderstorm.
>
> 那是一次强烈的雷暴。

---

> Participants were able to learn 20% faster after the intense running.
>
> 高强度跑步之后，参与者的学习速度提高了 20 %。

`Participants`  参与者

## **involve**

陷入什么处境、包括

> This man was involved in drunk driving accident. 
>
> 这个男人涉及到一起酒驾事故。

---

> Painting a house involves more trouble than she thought. 
>
> 粉刷一间房子比她想象的要包括更多的麻烦。 

## **dispite**

尽管

> Despite the fact that my parents hated her, I still asked her to marry me.
>
> 尽管事实上我的家人讨厌她，我仍然叫她嫁给我。

---

> It may lead to misunderstanding despite good intentions. 
>
> 这可能会导致误解，尽管意图是好的。

`intentions` 意图

`misunderstanding` 误会
